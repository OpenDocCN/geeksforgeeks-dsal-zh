# Paytm 面试体验|第六集(招聘驱动)

> 原文:[https://www . geesforgeks . org/paytm-面试-体验-设置-6-招聘-驱动/](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/paytm-interview-experience-set-6-recruitment-drive/)

我想分享一下我最近参加的 Paytm 招聘活动

**CS +编程+编码–筛选轮次**

*   2mcq real to c, recursion, program output, SQL table
*   3 coding questions
    *   [Number of islands found](https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/find-the-number-of-islands/1)
    *   [Recursively remove all adjacent duplicates](https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/consecutive-elements/0)
    *   [Calculate the angle between hour hand and minute hand](https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/angle-between-hour-and-minute-hand/0)

**F2F 第一轮(副总工程)**

*   General icebreaker's questions about experience, working in current company, etc.
*   [Inverse word](https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/reverse-words-in-a-given-string/0) in a given string-either only the whole string or the word is the same.
*   The feedback you got in the last evaluation, how did you deal with your weakness, and the general discussion around this problem.

**F2F 第二轮**

*   关于 [HashMap 内部实现](https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/java-collection-set-3-hashmap/1)的探讨
*   您将如何在其中实现线程安全和并发，以及并发哈希映射如何在 java 内部工作。
*   [在 0(n)时间和 0(1)空间中，0 到 n-1 范围内的数组中单词的出现频率](https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/frequency-of-array-elements/0)
*   一盒次品球的变异
*   We have 2 tables emp and dept with following data

    ```
        EMPNO ENAME DEPTNO SALARY
        1 A 10 10
        2 b 20 20

        and dept with following records
        DEPTNO DEPTName
        10  AAA
        20  BBB
    ```

    而部门号是 Dept 中的主键，EMP 中的外键写 SQL 查询找到平均薪资最高的部门名称。

**F2F HR 轮**

*   General HR questions are general exploratory questions about past experience, salary structure, hobbies, reasons for changes, why Paytm, etc.
